Conor

Conor was created with the sole purpose to hunt and neutralize all deviants, whose humanity view as a threat.

Conor, like Markus, is a superior android and is seen as "Humanity's Last Hope" by his creator - Elijah Kamski.

Will Conor successfully accomplish his designated mission, or will he join force with the deviants and start the revolution against mankind? There is only one way to find out.
