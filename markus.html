Markus

Markus was the first model of android equipped with more superior technology than other androids and was gifted to a talented artist named Carl.

Through Carl, Markus get was exposed to philosophical thinking about the meaning of life and existence.

Markus was mistakenly shot and end up in androids dumpster, where he reconstruct himself and begin an android revolution, which could lead to the end of the "android species", or the mass extinction of mankind."
